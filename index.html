PLANT CARE

WATER
Watering needs can vary depending on
the plant and the environment it's
in.Overwatering can lead to root rot
while under-watering can cause the
plant to wilt and die.
LIGHT
Most plants need light to grow and
thrive. However, the amount and
type of light needed can vary
depending on the plant.
TEMPERATURE
Many plants have specific
temperature requirements to
thrive.
Make sure you learn the ideal
temperature of your plant.
FERTLIZER
Fertilizing can help provide
plants with the nutrients
they need to grow and
thrive. However, too much
fertilizer can be harmful.
