Flowering Plants

Rose

Roses are one of the most popular flowering plants, known for their beautiful and fragrant blooms. They come in a wide variety of colors and can be grown in gardens or as cut flowers.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are large, vibrant flowers that are known for their sunny appearance. They are commonly grown for their beauty and as a source of edible seeds.

Tulip

Tulips are elegant flowers that come in a range of colors and shapes. They are often associated with spring and are widely planted in gardens and used as cut flowers.

Lily

Lilies are known for their large, showy flowers and sweet fragrance. They are available in various types, including Asiatic lilies, Oriental lilies, and Daylilies.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus plants produce vibrant and exotic-looking flowers. They are commonly grown in tropical and subtropical regions and can be found in different colors and sizes.
